Senior Executives
Meet the leadership team responsible for managing and developing Islamic and Arabic education across The Gambia.

Executive Committee
Executive Committee Members
The Executive Committee consists of directors of major institutes, the head of the Supreme Islamic Council, and advisers from educational institutions.

C
Chairman
Leads the Executive Committee and represents AMAANAH at the highest level
V
Vice Chairman
Supports the Chairman and assumes leadership in the Chairman's absence
E
Executive Director
Chief executive responsible for day-to-day operations and staff management
S
Secretary
Manages official correspondence and maintains institutional records
T
Treasurer
Oversees financial management and reporting to the Committee
Executive Bureau
Key Roles in the Executive Bureau
The Executive Bureau is the management hub of AMAANAH, ensuring policy execution, educational planning, and staff management.

Executive Director
Leadership
Provides overall leadership and coordination for internal operations and strategic decisions. Oversees day-to-day operations and implements Executive Committee resolutions.

Key Responsibilities:

•
Strategic leadership and vision implementation
•
Executive Committee liaison and reporting
•
Cross-functional coordination and oversight
•
Staff performance and development oversight
Administrative Secretary
Administration
Manages the secretariat and record office, ensuring accurate institutional records and supporting internal/external communication.

Key Responsibilities:

•
Correspondence and filing management
•
Internal communication facilitation
•
Documentation and records safeguarding
•
Administrative efficiency oversight
Head of Programmes & Operations
Programmes
Leads curriculum development, assessment, quality assurance, and monitoring activities across all affiliated institutions.

Key Responsibilities:

•
Curriculum development and training coordination
•
Examination and assessment oversight
•
Quality assurance and monitoring management
•
Data and certification administration
Public Relations Officer
Communications
Manages external relations, public image, communications, and information dissemination to stakeholders and the public.

Key Responsibilities:

•
External relations and stakeholder engagement
•
Media management and public campaigns
•
Institutional partnership coordination
•
Communication materials development
Human Resources Officer
Human Resources
Oversees human resource development, staff welfare, performance management, and HR policy compliance.

Key Responsibilities:

•
Recruitment and onboarding
•
Staff welfare and development
•
Performance management
•
HR policy implementation
Treasurer/Accounting Officer
Finance
Ensures accurate financial management, reporting, and compliance with financial policies and procedures.

Key Responsibilities:

•
Financial transaction management
•
Budget implementation and reporting
•
Audit coordination
•
Financial compliance oversight
Curriculum & Training Officer
Education
Leads curriculum design, review, and implementation while coordinating teacher training and professional development.

Key Responsibilities:

•
Curriculum design and alignment
•
Learning materials development
•
Teacher training coordination
•
Curriculum implementation monitoring
Assessment & Examinations Officer
Assessment
Oversees standardized testing, examination logistics, marking, and results analysis for all affiliated institutions.

Key Responsibilities:

•
Examination development and administration
•
Marking coordination and quality control
•
Results analysis and reporting
•
Examination policy compliance
Quality Assurance & Monitoring Officer
Quality
Coordinates national and regional monitoring, collects institutional data, and ensures compliance with education standards.

Key Responsibilities:

•
School monitoring and evaluation
•
Quality standards compliance
•
Institutional data collection
•
Performance improvement support
ICT Officer
Technology
Provides reliable ICT infrastructure, supports digital transformation, and maintains data systems across the organization.

Key Responsibilities:

•
IT systems maintenance
•
Digital platform management
•
Technical support and training
•
Data security and backup
Head of Endowment & Projects
Projects
Develops, implements, and manages funded projects, endowments, and resource mobilization initiatives.

Key Responsibilities:

•
Project development and management
